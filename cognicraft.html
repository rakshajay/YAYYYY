"This is an app developed for my Thesis @ SCI-Arc, California. This is a tech where neuroscience and design converge to redefine the shopping experience. Utilizing EEG technology, the project captures customers' brainwaves, translating their cognitive states into a tailored design language. This approach allows the retail environment to dynamically adapt to the collective emotions of its visitors, creating a shared yet personalized journey. Integrating EEG with AI algorithms, the store responds to individual preferences and moods, offering an immersive and customized experience. This app turns retail into more than just a transaction; it's an engaging, personal journey, where each moment is crafted to reflect the customer's inner world"
