"The 'Augmented Cities' design lab, as part of the Daegu Global Studio, explores the transformative role of AI in urban environments. Focusing on Daegu, South Korea, this project envisions the future of city living, work, and socialization through advanced technologies. We investigate specific technological aspects, like pneumatic waste systems, to reimagine future cityscapes and architecture. Each concept will creatively address Daegu's unique history, culture, and challenges. Our insights, set to be published and exhibited in late 2023, aim to influence urban planning in Daegu. This collaborative research, showcasing at an international symposium, has already garnered positive responses, promising inspiring, globally relevant urban solutions.

Project Team: Soomeen Hahm and Team @ SCI-Arc, California, 2023 "
