"Assetz Interior design crafts a 3BHK villa that's a testament to contemporary minimalism. Infusing soft balsa wood with sleek metal furnishings, the design integrates indoor greens, enhancing tranquility and connection to nature. The living room boasts wooden strip cladding that reaches 1.5 meters, accentuating its lofty height. A full mirror in the dining area reflects the double-height skylight, amplifying space and light. A bespoke wall shelf displays the client's travel souvenirs, personalizing the space. The child's bedroom, vibrant with their interests, adds playfulness. Overall, the design embodies the client's passions, complementing the architectural interplay of light and spatial dynamism."
