"Marvanthe Daba is a testament to simplicity, designed to blend with the untouched splendor of Maravanthe. This airy, shipping container-based structure is a minimalist's haven, set against a backdrop where the Arabian Sea meets the tranquil Souparnika River along NH-66. The design capitalizes on scenic drives, offering pit-stop tranquility with views of nature's dual canvases. It's a retreat strategically oriented for enjoying breathtaking sunsets and embracing the cooler, shaded interiors during the warm mornings. Every aspect celebrates the lightness of structure and the heaviness of the scene, ensuring unobstructed panoramic views that are as open and serene as Maravanthe itself."
