"This project transforms Sam McBratney's beloved children's book, 'Guess How Much I Love You,' into an immersive augmented reality experience.This project brings the story's characters to 3D life. Unity-driven, it links physical books with interactive digital content, transforming reading into an immersive, educational experience for children."
